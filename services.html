01 How I work with organisations

Human Integrity Advisory exists for situations where standard consulting and internal committees are not enough. Where AI, data, culture, and public trust intersect, you do not need another deck. You need someone who understands how systems actually behave, how power and fear distort decisions, and where integrity is silently leaking.

I combine three strands. Years spent close to neural networks and fraud architectures. Years translating research and complexity into products and structures that could operate in the real world. And years living on the receiving end of systems that failed to see their own impact. The work sits at the point where those strands meet.

If you are looking for reassurance, brand polish, or someone to endorse decisions already made, I am not a fit. If you want clear sight and are prepared to act on it, we may work well together.

02 Core service lines

Three primary ways organisations use me. Each format is contained, with clear scope and outcomes.

Boardroom

Boardroom Integrity Advisory

A confidential, independent mind in the room when the decision in front of you will be remembered.

  • Support on contested or high-stakes decisions where internal voices are conflicted or cautious.
  • A clear reading of the unseen dynamics shaping the choice in front of you.
  • Written synthesis that strips out spin and makes the actual trade-offs explicit.

Typical context: restructures, launches, scrutiny, governance failures, or fragile alignment between senior voices.

Request outline
Systems

AI & Systems Integrity Review

A focused examination of how one of your systems behaves for real people — especially those with the least power to contest it.

  • Mapping journeys through your AI and decision flows from the perspective of those affected.
  • Review of thresholds, uncertainty handling, and edge cases where harm accumulates.
  • Identification of failure modes and integrity gaps that do not appear on dashboards.

Often used for systems touching health, finance, benefits, identity, or any context where a wrong decision has real human cost.

Leaders

Executive Reflection & Integrity Labs

Structured space for senior leaders to think honestly without performance — with someone who is not trying to manage them.

  • One-to-one reflection for CEOs and senior leaders holding complex or isolating decisions.
  • Small Integrity Labs for senior teams when narrative and reality no longer match.
  • Written follow-ups that translate insight into clear next steps.

Useful when culture feels brittle, trust is thinning, or you sense something is off but cannot yet name it.

03 Engagement formats & fees

All work sits inside clear engagement formats. Fees reflect complexity, access, and responsibility. They are not negotiable on the basis of comfort.

Ongoing

Integrity Retainer

A named independent advisor for a board, founder, or senior team who wants integrity in view over time, not just at moments of crisis.

From £2,000 per month

Typical range £2,000 to £4,000 per month, adjusted for cadence, scope, and organisational complexity.

  • Scheduled advisory calls aligned with board cycles and key decision points.
  • Written synthesis after major decisions, capturing risks and unresolved questions.
  • Review of board papers, public statements, and accountability frameworks.

Best suited for organisations that want a consistent, independent lens on integrity — not a one-off intervention.

Ethics Review Sprint

£4,000–£6,000

04 Choosing the right format

The right format depends less on budget and more on what is actually at stake.

Single decision

When you are holding one decision you cannot afford to get wrong

An Executive Reflection Session is often the cleanest starting point. We clarify what is at stake, where pressure is coming from, what you are afraid to name, and what integrity would look like in practice.

System or product

When a system keeps raising questions

The Ethics Review Sprint provides a detailed but contained examination of how your AI or decision system behaves for real people — giving leadership, legal, and technical teams a shared, concrete picture of reality.

Ongoing view

When you want someone tracking integrity over time

An Integrity Retainer offers a stable relationship where someone consistently pays attention to how decisions, systems, and human impact line up over months and years — not just when something breaks in public.

05 Practicalities & working boundaries
  • Based in the United Kingdom. Working internationally through remote advisory and selected in-person sessions.
  • All work is treated as private and confidential. Any public reference or attribution is agreed separately and in writing.
  • Each engagement is supported by a written agreement covering scope, fees, timing, and review points.
  • I do not provide informal coaching, unscoped advice, or crisis containment without a clear agreement and defined boundaries.

These boundaries exist to protect decision makers, affected stakeholders, and the integrity of the work itself.
