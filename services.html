Services and Engagements

How I work with organisations

Human Integrity Advisory exists for moments where standard tools are not enough. Where AI, data, culture, and public trust intersect, leaders need more than a framework or an internal workshop. They need an independent mind in the room, clear sight of human impact, and written clarity they can stand behind.

The services below reflect how organisations use me when they want grounded thinking that bridges governance, technology, and lived reality. Each offer is deliberately contained, with clear scope, outcomes, and boundaries so that everyone understands what is and is not included.

All work is confidential and off record unless explicitly agreed otherwise in writing.

Scope and clarity

Every engagement begins with a written scope that defines responsibilities, communication channels, and boundaries. There is no informal support outside that structure. Additional questions, calls, or document reviews sit outside scope and are treated as new pieces of work with their own agreement. This protects the integrity of the work and the people affected by it.

Choosing the right format

If you are not sure where to begin, the guidance below reflects how leaders typically enter the work. The right format depends less on budget and more on the kind of problem you are actually holding.

Single decision

When you are holding a single heavy decision

An Executive Reflection Session is often the cleanest place to start. We clarify what is at stake, where pressure is coming from, and what integrity would look like in practice, then translate that into options you can act on quickly.

System or product

When a system or product is your main concern

The Ethics Review Sprint provides a detailed but contained examination of how your AI or decision system behaves for real people. It gives leadership, legal, and technical teams a shared, concrete starting point for change.

Ongoing view

When you want integrity in view over time

An Integrity Retainer offers a stable relationship where someone is consistently tracking how decisions, systems, and human impact line up over time, not just at moments of crisis or external scrutiny.

Practicalities and working boundaries

  • I am based in the United Kingdom and work internationally through remote advisory and selected in person sessions.
  • All work is treated as private and confidential. Any public reference or attribution is agreed separately and in writing.
  • Each engagement is supported by a written agreement that captures scope, fees, timing, and review points.
  • I do not provide informal coaching, unscoped advice, or crisis containment without a clear agreement.

This structure exists to protect decision makers, affected stakeholders, and the integrity of the work itself.
