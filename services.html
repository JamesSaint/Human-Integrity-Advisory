Services and Engagements

I work with boards, founders, and senior leaders when decisions carry real risk and it is no longer safe to rely on optimistic narratives or internal reassurance.

My background includes building fraud systems, co-founding a research driven platform that was commercially acquired, and sitting inside the consequences of institutional failure. I now use that experience as an independent counterpart who is paid to tell you what others will not.

How I work with organisations

Human Integrity Advisory exists for situations where standard consulting and internal committees are not enough. Where AI, data, culture, and public trust intersect, you do not need another deck. You need someone who understands how systems actually behave, how power and fear distort decisions, and where integrity is silently leaking.

I combine three strands. Years spent close to neural networks and fraud architectures. Years translating research and complexity into products and structures that could operate in the real world. And years living on the receiving end of systems that failed to see their own impact. The work sits at the point where those strands meet.

If you are looking for reassurance, brand polish, or someone to endorse decisions already made, I am not a fit. If you want clear sight and are prepared to act on it, we may work well together.

Scope and clarity

Every engagement begins with a written scope that defines responsibilities, communication channels, and boundaries. There is no informal support outside that structure. Additional questions, calls, or document reviews sit outside scope and are treated as new work with their own agreement. This protects the integrity of the work and everyone involved.

Choosing the right format

If you are unsure where to begin, the guidance below reflects how leaders typically enter the work. The right format depends less on budget and more on what is actually at stake.

Single decision

When you are holding one decision you cannot afford to get wrong

An Executive Reflection Session is often the cleanest starting point. We clarify what is at stake, where pressure is coming from, what you are afraid to name, and what integrity would look like in practice. You leave with a written view you can test your choices against.

System or product

When a system keeps raising questions

The Ethics Review Sprint provides a detailed but contained examination of how your AI or decision system behaves for real people. It gives leadership, legal, and technical teams a shared, concrete picture of reality rather than a set of assumptions.

Ongoing view

When you want someone tracking integrity over time

An Integrity Retainer offers a stable relationship where someone consistently pays attention to how decisions, systems, and human impact line up over months and years, not just when something breaks in public.

Practicalities and working boundaries

  • I am based in the United Kingdom and work internationally through remote advisory and selected in person sessions.
  • All work is treated as private and confidential. Any public reference or attribution is agreed separately and in writing.
  • Each engagement is supported by a written agreement covering scope, fees, timing, and review points.
  • I do not provide informal coaching, unscoped advice, or crisis containment without a clear agreement and defined boundaries.

These boundaries exist to protect decision makers, affected stakeholders, and the integrity of the work itself.
