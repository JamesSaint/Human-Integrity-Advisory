## Executive Summary

Human Integrity Advisory is an independent strategic advisory practice providing **AI risk governance, ethical oversight, and decision-quality frameworks** for boards, investors, and senior leadership teams. This repository contains the public website hosted at:

**https://jamessaint.github.io/Human-Integrity-Advisory/**

The aim is to offer a high-trust, high-clarity digital presence that communicates the advisory’s value to organisations navigating AI transformation, regulatory complexity, and human-centred risk.

## Why This Advisory Exists

Leadership teams are facing unprecedented pressure:  
- Emerging AI regulation  
- Ethical and reputational risk  
- Stakeholder scrutiny  
- Board-level accountability for safety and governance  
- Increased complexity in decision environments  

Human Integrity Advisory supports organisations by strengthening the human, ethical, and governance foundations behind these decisions.

## Core Advisory Areas

- **AI Governance and Safety Oversight**  
  Board-level guidance on risk, safeguards, and organisational readiness.

- **Ethical Decision Frameworks**  
  Structure for complex or high-impact decisions.

- **Integrity-Based Leadership**  
  Helping leaders navigate crises, transitions, and cultural alignment.

- **Strategic Advisory to Investors**  
  Due diligence on AI maturity, leadership integrity, and risk exposure.

- **Independent Perspective**  
  A neutral, non-political lens during high-stakes evaluation.
