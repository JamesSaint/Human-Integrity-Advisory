01 Why work with me

Most organisations have values on the wall and a risk register on the server. Fewer have a clear view of what really happens in the room when money, power, technology, and human lives meet under pressure.

I work with boards and senior leadership teams who want clarity, not theatre. My focus is human integrity in practice: how decisions are made and justified, how AI and data systems are designed and deployed, and how all of this lands in the nervous systems and daily lives of real people.

I am not a brand story on a slide. I am an independent mind in the room who will name what your people and your customers will feel but may never say.

05 How I work

My work is grounded, confidential, and deliberately free from performance. I am not a trainer or a keynote speaker. I am a long-view ally whose only stake is the integrity of the work and the lives it touches.

What I am not — I am not a box-ticking compliance consultant. I am not a PR shield for decisions that are already misaligned. I am not available to justify what you already know is indefensible.

06 Questions I help boards hold
  • Where does our current AI or data strategy quietly contradict the values we say we stand for?
  • What are we asking our most vulnerable users or customers to absorb on our behalf?
  • What would it look like for this decision to be one we are still proud of in ten years' time?
  • What happens to trust if our next system failure becomes public before we have spoken about it honestly?

From there we move from questions to a concrete plan: specific risks, clear trade-offs, and pragmatic steps that respect regulatory reality, commercial constraints, and human lives together.
